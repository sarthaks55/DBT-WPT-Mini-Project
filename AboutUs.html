About PTMS

Revolutionizing Property Tax Management with Technology & Trust

Our Story

Founded in 2020, PTMS was born from a simple observation: property tax management was unnecessarily complex, time-consuming, and error-prone. Property owners struggled with tracking multiple deadlines, municipalities faced inefficiencies, and the entire process lacked transparency.

We set out to change that. Our team of tax experts, software engineers, and financial professionals came together to build a platform that simplifies property tax compliance while ensuring accuracy and security.

Today, PTMS serves thousands of property owners, housing societies, and municipal corporations across India, processing millions in tax payments annually and helping communities stay compliant effortlessly.
